# Nuuk temperature data

Annual summaries of monthly mean temperatures in Nuuk from 1867 to 2013.

## Usage

``` r
nuuk
```

## Format

A data frame with 147 rows and 6 columns:

- Year:

  Year (numeric)

- Temperature:

  Annual mean temperature

- Median:

  Annual median of montly mean temperatures

- High:

  Annual maximum of montly mean temperatures

- Low:

  Annual minimum of montly mean temperatures

- Range:

  High - Low
