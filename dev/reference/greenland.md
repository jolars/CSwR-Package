# South west Greenland temperature data

Monthly average temperatures in degree Celsius in Nuuk and Qaqortoq from
1873 to 2013.

## Usage

``` r
greenland
```

## Format

A data frame with 147 rows and 5 columns:

- Year:

  Year (numeric)

- Month:

  Month (encoded as a factor with levels 1 to 12)

- Temp_nuuk:

  Montly mean temperature in Nuuk

- Temp_Qaqortoq:

  Montly mean temperature in Qaqortoq

- Temp_diff:

  Temperature difference, `Temp_nuuk - Temp_Qaqortoq`
